# Creation Spec

## Core Principle

Every draft should feel native to Xiaohongshu and specific to the repo's brand context.

## Creation Checklist

1. Confirm audience pain point.
2. Confirm brand point of view.
3. Choose one clear angle only.
4. Make the first screen worth stopping for.
5. Keep the body scannable and concrete.
6. Use a CTA that matches the offer and trust level.

## Output Requirements

- One primary angle per note
- One primary audience segment per note
- Concrete scenario, example, or observation
- No generic filler paragraphs
- No claims unsupported by repo knowledge
- If information is still missing, preserve `<placeholder>...</placeholder>` instead of vague filler

## Draft Quality Bar

- Hook creates curiosity or immediate relevance
- Body has forward motion
- CTA feels proportionate and believable
- Tone matches `brand/tone.md`
- No taboo expression from `brand/taboo.md`
